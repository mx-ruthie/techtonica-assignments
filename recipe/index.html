Chicken and Matzoh Ball Soup for the Soul

Everyone knows that chicken noodle soup cures all sorts of ailments, but Jewish people have an extra special ingredient: Matzoh Balls! We always make this soup in my family when someone feels ill (body or soul) and it helps right away. Follow along for a traditional take on this cultural staple.

  • Whole Chickem (4-5 lbs.)
  • 1 lb. carrots (whole peeled and chopped, or baby carrots)
  • 1 lb. celery (chopped)
